Roses

Roses have thorns
Violets are blue
The devil has horns
And so do you

Sure they may be pretty
But pretty won't keep you warm at night
So forgive me if I'm petty
But I want more than "pretty" for my life

Water

You're like
Water
On my skin.
Suffocating, but welcome.
Cleansing, but uncomfortable
Drying me out as you evaporate
And go away
Leaving me here wondering...
If I would have been better off
Never having known you at all
